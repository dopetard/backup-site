About

Ankur Kumar is an undergraduate student in Wuhan University majoring in Computer Science. He originally hails from India. His frontend design skills are as bad as his batting, which in turn, ain't bad at all. Apart from implicitly being preposterous about his superhuman Cricketing skills, Ankur is a Machine learning enthusiast who prefers to refer to himself in third person. When not optimizing his Neural Networks, Ankur is busy hanging out with friends and perfecting his poker-face.

The animal in this picture above is a llama. It's Ankur's spirit animal. True Story.

Work

Ankur worked in Chinese Academy of sciences as Research intern, building Generative Adversarial Nerowrks. He learned different ways to better train a GAN. He also compared GAN with many other unsupervised Deep learning techniques like Autoencoders. He is also very passionate about giving back to the community and often writes Medium blogs about his learnings.
